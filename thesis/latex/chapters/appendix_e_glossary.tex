
This appendix provides definitions for the key geospatial, economic, and machine learning terms utilized throughout this thesis.

\begin{table}[h]
\centering
\begin{tabular}{ll}
\toprule
Term & Definition \\
\midrule
\textbf{Albedo} & The measure of the diffuse reflection of solar radiation out of the total solar radiation received by an object or surface (e.g., snow and salt flats exhibit high albedo). \\
\textbf{Backscatter} & The portion of the microwave signal reflected back to the radar antenna from objects on the ground (critical for Sentinel-1 SAR analysis). \\
\textbf{Bands} & Discrete intervals of the electromagnetic spectrum captured by satellite sensors (e.g., Red, Green, Blue, NIR). \\
\textbf{Cloud Mask} & A binary classification raster marking which pixels are obstructed by clouds or cloud shadows, permitting cleaner analysis. \\
\textbf{Contamination Rate} & The expected proportion of outliers/anomalies in a dataset, used as a threshold parameter in Isolation Forests. \\
\textbf{Granule} & A single tile or scene of satellite imagery packaged for distribution by data portals. \\
\textbf{Isolation Forest} & An unsupervised machine learning algorithm that isolates anomalies by partitioning features using random decision trees. \\
\textbf{Radiance} & The measure of the quantity of electromagnetic radiation that leaves or passes through a particular point, measured in $\text{nW}\cdot\text{cm}^{-2}\cdot\text{sr}^{-1}$. \\
\textbf{SWIR (Shortwave-Infrared)} & Electromagnetic bands in the range of 1.4 to 3.0 microns, highly sensitive to moisture levels and soil exposure. \\
\textbf{Volume Scattering} & The reflection of radar waves in multiple directions due to structural objects like tree branches, which drops dramatically during deforestation. \\
\textbf{Z-Score} & A statistical metric representing the number of standard deviations an observation is from the mean of a baseline dataset. \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{table}
