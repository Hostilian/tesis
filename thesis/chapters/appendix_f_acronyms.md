# Appendix F: List of Acronyms

*   **API**: Application Programming Interface

*   **CDSE**: Copernicus Data Space Ecosystem

*   **CSU**: Czech Statistical Office (Český statistický úřad)
*   **DNB**: Day/Night Band
*   **DSS**: Decision Support System
*   **EAR**: Export Administration Regulations
*   **EO**: Earth Observation
*   **ESA**: European Space Agency
*   **ETL**: Extract, Transform, Load
*   **GDP**: Gross Domestic Product
*   **GEE**: Google Earth Engine
*   **GIS**: Geographic Information System
*   **GDPR**: General Data Protection Regulation
*   **ITAR**: International Traffic in Arms Regulations
*   **KII**: Department of Informatics (Katedra informačního inženýrství)
*   **ML**: Machine Learning
*   **MSI**: Multi-Spectral Instrument (Sentinel-2)
*   **NDVI**: Normalized Difference Vegetation Index
*   **NDWI**: Normalized Difference Water Index
*   **NIR**: Near-Infrared
*   **NTL**: Night-time Lights
*   **OLI**: Operational Land Imager (Landsat)
*   **PEF**: Faculty of Economics and Management (Provozně ekonomická fakulta)
*   **ROI**: Region of Interest
*   **SAR**: Synthetic Aperture Radar
*   **SPA**: Single Page Application
*   **SWIR**: Shortwave-Infrared
*   **USGS**: United States Geological Survey
*   **VIIRS**: Visible Infrared Imaging Radiometer Suite
*   **WCAG**: Web Content Accessibility Guidelines
